Dwarf Fortress Launcher is just what is sounds like.
Click "Launch DF" in the top right to play.

Backup and restore saves and the DF configs in the Backup and Restore section.
Add fonts or tilesets to the data directory to select them in the Font and Tilesets section.
Modify DF configs in the Settings section.

Most items have a tooltip if you hover over them.

Note: install Dwarf Fortress through preferences to be able to launch.
